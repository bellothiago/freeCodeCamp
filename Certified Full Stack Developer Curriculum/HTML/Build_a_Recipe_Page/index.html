Simple Grilled Steak Recipe

A perfectly grilled steak doesn’t need much—just quality meat, salt, pepper, and some olive oil. This straightforward method gives you a juicy, flavorful steak with minimal fuss, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a weekend treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 steak (ribeye, sirloin, or your choice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Take the steak out of the fridge 30-60 min before grilling. Pat dry.
  2. Rub both sides with salt, pepper, and a little olive oil.
  3. Preheat grill to high heat.
  4. Grill steak:
    • Rare: 2-3 min per side
    • Medium-rare: 3-4 min per side
    • Medium: 4-5 min per side
  5. Remove steak and let rest 5-10 min before slicing.
